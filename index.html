Our Story

From Europe to Asia: A journey of digital innovation

2014 - Founded in Europe

Meridian Works was born with a simple mission: to empower businesses through exceptional web design and development. What started as a small team in Europe grew into a trusted partner for companies looking to establish their digital presence.

2019 - Discovering a Digital Gap

After years of success in Europe, our team noticed a critical pattern: many promising businesses in Southeast Asia and Oceania lacked a strong digital presence. Despite having excellent products and services, they struggled to reach customers online. This observation sparked a crucial question: How could we help?

2020-2024 - Expansion to Asia-Pacific

We made the bold decision to expand to Southeast Asia and Oceania. What we found was remarkable: businesses hungry for growth, ready to embrace digital transformation, but lacking access to quality web development services. We opened offices, built local partnerships, and began our mission to bridge the digital divide.

Today - Your Digital Partner

Today, Meridian Works operates as a trusted web development agency across Europe, Asia, and Oceania. We've helped hundreds of businesses of all sizes launch successful online platforms. From startups to established companies, our mission remains unchanged: create beautiful, functional websites that drive real business growth.
